About Me

Hey there! I'm Gerard!

I'm a software engineer based in Houston, TX and I build fantastic looking websites and web applications. Technology has always been one of my passions and through coding, I am able to express my creativity and problem solving. Let's work together to see how I can help you.

My Services

I help my clients by building projects that help achieve their goals. Communication and knowing what to expect during our time together is crucial in achieving our mutual goal of success. Here is how we will collaborate:

  • 1. Consultation

    • Our first step begins with a simple conversation. We will discuss what goals and ideas you have about the project you have in mind and the best approach to complete it.

  • 2. Proposal

    • Our next step is having a detailed proposal of the project which includes a visual preview of the potential final outcome. We will then discuss what our plan of action will be.

  • 3. Creation

    • The third step is the creation of the project. Clients will receive weekly updates about the project as I create and test it.

  • 4. Launch and Support

    • Finally, the project will be finalized and approved by the client. It will be launched and ongoing support will be available if needed.

Projects

Here are some of my recent projects

Electric Glow

Full stack web application enabling users to effortlessly log in, track, and share images from their cherished festivals and music events. Implemented a robust database system to store the tracked information, accompanied by an interactive map showcasing the venue location, user-shared images, and their memorable moments.

Med Tracks

Highly efficient medication tracker web application leveraging the power of React. Empowers users to seamlessly input their current medication time and set personalized reminders for the next dose. Utilizes React's hooks and state management to efficiently store data locally, ensuring a smooth user experience. Additionally, incorporated a convenient reminder feature for users opting to receive timely notifications.

Pediatric Vitals

Educational reference web application utilized by nursing students and new hospital staff which returns pediatric vital signs. Utilizes an Independently built REST API containing parameters for the vital signs.

Itadori's Boba Shop

A fully responsive, modern, and simple yet elegant website for a boba shop.
